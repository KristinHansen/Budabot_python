<header2>Fountain of Life Perk<end>
<a href='itemref://302907/302907/302907'>Fountain of Life</a> will be available to use if a full set of Awakened Armor is worn.

The following setup will qualify as a "full set" worn:
<highlight>Helmet, Chest, Gloves, Shoulder, Back, Legs, Boots<end> and one of the following: <highlight>Ranged Sleeve or Melee Sleeve<end>

<a href='itemref://244750/244750/244750'>Sigil of Samael</a> is <red>NOT REQUIRED<end> to qualify for the "full set" bonus

To unlock the perk, you must right-click <a href='itemref://244718/244718/244718'>Awakened Burden of Competence</a>

NB: <a href='itemref://302907/302907/302907'>Fountain of Life</a> can't be looked up in this guide, before it has been unlocked by right-clicking <a href='itemref://244718/244718/244718'>Awakened Burden of Competence</a> (this can be done as soon as you have the armor piece). <a href='itemref://302907/302907/302907'>Fountain of Life</a> will be available from the "Programs"-window under "Medical".
